About Batches

A batch is a collection of products to optimize together.

After creating a batch, you can add products and their required amounts, then run the optimization to find the most economical mix of materials.

The optimization takes into account:

  • Composition requirements for each product
  • Available scrap materials and their costs
  • Total amount needed for each product
